I am a Software Engineering student at the **University of Brasília (UnB)** with a strong background in Physics, which I studied for two years before transitioning to engineering. My technical interests are broad and deeply rooted in both the theoretical and practical aspects of computation.

I have a deep passion for **Mathematics**, particularly Algebra and Calculus, which fuels my interest in **Algorithms and Data Structures**. I am fascinated by **Programming Language Compilers** and the inner workings of software. On the hardware side, I enjoy exploring **Electronics** and **Embedded Software**.

I am a polyglot programmer and enjoy working with a variety of paradigms. My toolkit includes **C++**, **Python**, **Prolog**, **APL**, **Haskell**, **Go**, **TypeScript**, and **Lua**. I am also an active participant in **Competitive Programming**.

Currently, I work as an **IT Banking Technician** at **CAIXA ECONÔMICA FEDERAL**, where my focus is on **Cybersecurity**. My professional and academic interests are currently converging on **Cryptography** and **Post-Quantum Cryptography**.

I am a strong advocate for **Open Source Software** and always looking for opportunities to contribute to the community.
